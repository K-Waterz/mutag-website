About MUTAG HOUSE

MUTAG HOUSE is a software development studio specialising in the design and delivery of scalable digital systems. We build the platforms, applications, and backend infrastructure that organisations rely on to operate, grow, and scale.

Our work goes beyond websites. We architect and implement custom web applications, secure authentication systems, APIs, dashboards, and automated workflows that support real business processes and complex operational requirements. Every system we build is grounded in clean architecture, clear business logic, and long-term maintainability.

We work with startups, SMEs, corporates, and institutions that require more than surface-level digital solutions. Our clients come to us when they need systems that integrate with existing tools, enforce access control, manage data securely, and perform reliably at scale.

While MUTAG HOUSE maintains capabilities in branding and digital marketing, these services exist to support the systems we build — not replace them. Our focus is on engineering digital infrastructure that works, lasts, and delivers measurable business impact.

At our core, we are builders of systems — practical, scalable, and designed for the realities of modern business.

Our Approach

Systems Architecture

We design and build software with clean architecture, scalability, and maintainability at the core. Every system is architected to handle growth, integrate seamlessly with existing infrastructure, and meet your long-term business needs.

What We Do

At MUTAG HOUSE, we offer a comprehensive suite of software development services designed to help your business succeed in today's digital landscape. Our core services include:

  • Custom Web Application Development: Full-stack web applications built with modern, production-ready frameworks—from Progressive Web Apps to multi-tenant SaaS platforms and enterprise portals.
  • Backend Systems & APIs: Scalable, distributed system architecture, RESTful and GraphQL APIs, database design, and third-party integrations.
  • Authentication & Security Systems: User authentication, OAuth, SSO, role-based access control, security audits, and compliance solutions.
  • Business Intelligence & Dashboards: Custom dashboards, real-time analytics platforms, data visualization, and KPI tracking systems.
  • Automation & Workflow Systems: Business process automation, workflow management, and integration with CRMs, ERPs, and business tools.
  • E-Commerce & Transaction Platforms: Custom e-commerce solutions, payment gateway integrations, inventory management, and subscription platforms.

We also offer supporting digital and marketing services—including branding, web development, and growth support—to ensure your software systems align with your brand identity and market positioning. Whether you're building your first software system, modernizing existing infrastructure, or scaling operations, we have the expertise and resources to help you achieve your goals.
